## Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze mobile sales performance, customer behavior, and transaction trends across different time periods and payment methods.

---

##  Purpose

The Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales data, helping users track revenue, analyze trends, and understand customer purchasing behavior.

This dashboard is useful for business analysts, sales managers, and decision-makers to monitor performance and optimize strategies.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

-  **Power BI Desktop** – Data visualization and dashboard creation  
-  **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation  
-  **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculations and KPIs  
-  **Data Modeling** – Relationships between tables  
-  **File Formats** – `.pbix`, `.png`  

---

##  Data Source

- Sample Mobile Sales Dataset
-  https://github.com/SatishDhawale/Power_BI_Dashboard/blob/main/Mobile%20Sales%20Data.xlsx

The dataset includes:
- Sales transactions  
- Payment methods (Cash, Card, Online)  
- Monthly and yearly sales data  
- Customer purchasing behavior  

---

###  Business Problem

Businesses often struggle to:
- Track monthly and yearly sales trends  
- Understand customer purchasing patterns  
- Identify preferred payment methods  
- Monitor revenue growth effectively  

---

###  Goal of the Dashboard

- Provide clear insights into mobile sales performance  
- Enable data-driven decision making  
- Identify trends and patterns in transactions  
- Improve sales strategy and customer targeting  

---

###  Key Visuals

- ** Time-based Analysis**
  - Monthly and yearly sales trends  

- ** Transactions by Payment Method**
  - Distribution of payments (Cash, Card, Online)  

- ** Customer Ratings Analysis**
  - Ratings breakdown (Good, Average, Poor)  

- ** Sales KPIs**
  - Total Sales  
  - Revenue trends  
  - Transaction volume  

- ** MTD (Month-To-Date) Analysis**
  - Performance tracking by Year, Quarter, Month  

---

###  Business Impact & Insights

-  **Sales Optimization**
  - Identify high-performing months and seasons  

- **Payment Insights**
  - Understand customer payment preferences  

- **Customer Understanding**
  - Analyze ratings and improve service  

- **Strategic Decisions**
  - Support business growth with data insights
